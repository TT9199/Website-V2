Westminster Council

Westminster City Council is the local authority for the City of Westminster, responsible for delivering essential services, supporting local communities, and maintaining one of London’s most historic and vibrant areas. With 54 elected councillors representing 18 wards, the council oversees key services such as housing, planning, waste collection, social care, and public health, serving a resident population of around 209,000 while supporting over 55,000 businesses and a daily population that can exceed one million. Its mission is to keep Westminster fair, inclusive, and sustainable for everyone.

About us

Westminster City Council is dedicated to serving one of the most vibrant, diverse, and historic communities in the UK. Our mission is to support residents, businesses, and visitors by delivering high‑quality public services, championing local opportunity, and protecting the unique character of our city.
